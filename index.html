US Economy 2026

A visual analysis — 2026 in the context of the past 25 years

This site is an interactive drill-down analysis of the US economy in 2026 placed against the past 25 years (2001–2025). Use the top navigation to move between the four panels, or click the links below.

Prototype dataset. Historical values are hand-entered from public sources (BEA, BLS, Federal Reserve, NBER, public market indices). Values for 2025 and 2026 are illustrative — they are not forecasts. See the About page for regeneration instructions and how to swap in live FRED data.
